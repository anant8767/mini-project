About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad was established in 1986 with the vision to provide quality education in the field of computer science and engineering.

Our department offers B.Tech and M.Tech programs that are designed to equip students with the theoretical foundation and practical skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer science.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we strive to create an environment that fosters innovation, research, and holistic development of our students.
